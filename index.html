Developer Skills and Learning Pathways: An Evidence-Based Framework for Strategic Human Resource Development

Preprint You, H. S. (2025)

This study presents a comprehensive, interpretable machine learning analysis of the 2024 Stack Overflow Developer Survey to investigate the market value of developer skills and the effectiveness of various learning pathways. Employing XGBoost models and SHAP value interpretation, we estimate skill premiums, value impacts, and learning method ROI across developer subgroups. Results reveal optimal learning approaches for different career stages and technical domains, providing a data-driven framework for strategic human resource development.

A Relationship among Self-direction, Teaching Presence, Social Presence and Perceived Academic Achievement of Teachers in Distance Training Program

Journal Article Kim, J. J., You, H. S. (2022)

This research examines the relationship between self-direction, teaching presence, social presence, and perceived academic achievement in distance training programs for teachers. The study provides insights into the factors that influence effective distance education, especially in the context of teacher professional development during the COVID-19 pandemic.
